# Branding & Theme — Faith Baptist Church of Chelsea

The game must be visually branded for **Faith Baptist Church of Chelsea**, not generic Jeopardy.

## Logo

- `assets/fbc-logo.png` — the church logo (cross + open Bible, "FAITH BAPTIST CHURCH of Chelsea").
- Display the logo on:
  - the **setup / intro screen** (large, centered)
  - the **board view** (small, in a corner or header — must not crowd the game grid)
  - the **Final Jeopardy "think" screen** (centered, with think music)
  - the **winner / end-game screen**
- Keep the logo on a white or very light background where shown (it has a white-ish backdrop and reads best that way). Do not place the full-color logo on a dark category tile where it would look boxed-in; use a white panel/header behind it.

## Exact brand colors (sampled from the logo)

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary slate gray | `#616a70` | main text / structure color (the "FAITH" letters & cross) |
| Brand blue | `#0093ce` | primary accent (the "BAPTIST CHURCH" text & bright Bible pages) |
| Deep blue | `#008fcc` | secondary blue accent / gradients |
| Light gray | `#bdc2c9` | the ribbon / subtle borders, muted UI |
| White | `#ffffff` | backgrounds / negative space |

Suggested usage:
- **Category header tiles:** slate gray `#616a70` background, white text.
- **Dollar-value clue tiles:** brand blue `#0093ce` background, white value text. Hover/selected: deep blue `#008fcc`.
- **Revealed clue screen:** white or very light background, slate-gray text for high readability when projected; brand-blue accents.
- **Scores / team colors:** teams can still pick their own colors, but the default UI chrome (buttons, headers, borders) should use the brand palette above.
- **Buzz-in indicator:** large, high-contrast — brand blue flash + the buzzer number in slate gray on white, or invert for visibility. Make sure it's readable across a room.

Keep contrast high and fonts large — this is projected for a room of people. Accessibility/readability beats decoration.
